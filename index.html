online.tess.science

8–10 September, 2020, UTC

A 48-hour international collaboration & co-working event focused on NASA's TESS Mission.

This program is intended to encourage new collaborations and facilitate the development of open-source software for learning from the TESS data. Over the 48 hours of this event (don't worry, you won't be expected to be working all that time!), you will collaborate on a project with other astronomers from around the world with the goals of learning something new, meeting people with complementary skills, and creating something useful (in the form of a software package, a paper, an idea, or something else that we haven't thought of yet).

If this is something that you're interested in, please fill out this form and we'll keep you posted on the details.
