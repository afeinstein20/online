Break Bingo

Goal: The goal of this activity is to encourage all participants to meet a variety of different people, especially beyond the group that they are working with.

Your job is to meet people with the following characteristics. Please wander around the Yotribe room, join a group, introduce yourself (full name, affiliation, and location), then see if anyone in the group has one of the attributes on your card. When you find someone who has one of the attributes on your card, click on that square to mark it as complete. If your card shows any of these winning patterns, let your circle know and feel pride wash over you!
